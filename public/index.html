FAQ

Why does my key expire?
Keys are valid for 24 hours from the moment you use them in the script. Come back to this site each day to generate a new one.
Can I share my key?
No. The key binds to your HWID (PC identifier) on first use. If someone tries to use it on another PC, it will be rejected.
Is it really free?
Yes, completely free. You can generate one key per day per IP. Keys reset every 24 hours.
Which features require a key?
The Fire ALL Remote (ID Blaster) requires a valid key. All other features are available without a key.
Why do I have to go through a verification?
The Workink verification step prevents automated abuse and keeps the key system free for everyone. It only takes a few seconds.
